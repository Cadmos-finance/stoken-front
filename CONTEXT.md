# Stoken Public Website

This context captures the domain language used by the Stoken public website so copy and navigation stay consistent across pages.

## Language

**Stoken**:
A funding instrument used to fund working capital needs that enable the production, transport, storage and delivery of commodities to their ultimate users.
_Avoid_: Generic commodity-trade-finance loan, repackaged pool

**Specimen facility**:
An illustrative, non-solicitation example of a single Stoken facility with sample rating, yield, tenor, currency, ticket and structure terms.
_Avoid_: The Terms, sample terms table

**Credit rating**:
A facility risk assessment assigned before investor capital is committed using Stoken scoring, Moody's RiskCalc baseline EDF-X dataset overlaid with Stoken's proprietary scoring methodology, or both.
_Avoid_: Calling this a public bond rating or implying investor capital is committed before rating assignment.

**Origination quality**:
The team-level review of issuer credit history, counterparty relationships, loan book and trade flows by practitioners with 50+ years of commodity finance experience gained across world-leading commodity finance banks.
_Avoid_: Individual biography, single-founder experience

**Accountability framework**:
The Swiss-law, regulated-intermediation and wallet-whitelisting structure that governs Stoken facility access and enforcement.
_Avoid_: Designed for capital

**Purpose-built payment contract**:
The contract that receives coupon payments on each payment date and distributes them to whitelisted investors.
_Avoid_: Stoken receives coupons, purpose-build contract

**Swiss arbitration**:
The dispute and enforcement framework under the Swiss Rules of International Arbitration that issuers and investors expressly consent to through a choice of law agreement.
_Avoid_: Generic Swiss jurisdiction

**Cloistered capital markets**:
The separated commodity finance and on-chain capital markets that Stoken bridges.
_Avoid_: Real goods and real capital meet on-chain

**Practitioners**:
The Stoken team members with direct commodity finance, legal, regulatory or on-chain capital-market experience.
_Avoid_: Veterans, manned

**Registered office**:
Stoken S.A., Av. de Champel 29, 1206 Genève, Switzerland.
_Avoid_: Geneva, Switzerland

## Relationships

- A **Stoken** funds working capital needs linked to commodity production, transport, storage or delivery.
- A **Specimen facility** is illustrative only and is not a live offer or solicitation.
- A **Specimen facility** represents one borrower and one facility; it is not a repackaged pool.
- A **Credit rating** is assigned before investor capital is committed.
- Rating copy can refer to Stoken scoring, Moody's RiskCalc baseline EDF-X dataset overlaid with Stoken's proprietary scoring methodology, or both.
- **Origination quality** is assessed before any facility is offered to investors.
- The **Accountability framework** governs access, issuance and enforcement language across the public website.
- A **Purpose-built payment contract** distributes coupon payments to whitelisted investors.
- **Swiss arbitration** applies to disputes and enforcement for Stoken issuances.
- **Cloistered capital markets** refers to commodity finance on one side and on-chain capital on the other.
- **Practitioners** is the canonical team-positioning term on the About page.
- The **Registered office** appears in footer legal text and structured data.

## Flagged ambiguities

- "The Terms" was used for the Asset-page specimen preview; resolved: the canonical public label is **Specimen facility**.
- "RiskCalc" and "rating paths" were reintroduced for the What we do rating copy; resolved: public copy can describe the **Credit rating** as assigned using Stoken scoring, Moody's RiskCalc baseline EDF-X dataset overlaid with Stoken's proprietary scoring methodology, or both.
- "25+ years" and "50+ years" refer to different scopes; resolved: **Origination quality** uses team-level 50+ years, while individual team biographies may keep individual experience claims.
- "Designed for capital" was used for the Swiss framework headline; resolved: the public positioning is **Accountability framework** language.
- "Stoken receives coupons" was used for coupon flow; resolved: coupon payments are received by a **Purpose-built payment contract**.
- Swiss-law copy previously only mentioned choice of law; resolved: include **Swiss arbitration** for disputes and enforcement.
- "Real goods and real capital meet on-chain" was removed from About copy; resolved: use **Cloistered capital markets** language instead.
- "Veterans" and "manned" were considered for team positioning; resolved: use **Practitioners**.
- Footer and structured-data address copy used only "Geneva, Switzerland"; resolved: use the full **Registered office**.
