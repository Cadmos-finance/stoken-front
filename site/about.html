About · Geneva, Switzerland

Where real goods and real capital meet on-chain.

Stoken S.A. bridges the $9T commodity trade finance market and institutional on-chain capital under Swiss law.

Mission

Stoken exists to bridge two of the world's most significant capital markets. On one side, a $9 trillion annual commodity trade finance market that powers the movement of physical goods across the globe, historically accessible only to the world's largest trade finance banks.

On the other, hundreds of billions in institutional on-chain capital actively seeking real-economy yield, uncorrelated to crypto markets and backed by genuine economic activity.

Stoken connects the two, bringing independently rated, short-term commodity trade finance facilities to on-chain investors, under Swiss law and with full on-chain transparency.

Vision

The tokenization of real-world assets is not a trend but a structural shift in how capital markets operate. Commodity trade finance, one of the oldest and most fundamental asset classes in global finance, deserves to be part of that shift.

Stoken's vision is to become the institutional standard for on-chain commodity credit, a platform where every facility is independently rated, every transaction is verifiable on-chain, and every investor operates within a clear and well-established legal framework.

A market where real goods and real capital meet on-chain.
