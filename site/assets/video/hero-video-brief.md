# Stoken Hero Video Brief

Use this brief to generate or review the homepage hero video candidate.

## Prompt

Create the first segment of a photorealistic cinematic hero background video for an institutional commodity finance website.

The video should show the beginning of a global commodity supply-chain story in a premium documentary style. Start with daytime agricultural production: open fields, harvested crops or raw commodities, practical farm logistics, trucks or rail beginning to move goods inland, and a transition toward storage and processing infrastructure.

Show storage silos, warehouses, bulk handling systems, and organized commodity inventory. The segment should feel operational, credible, and finance-grade, with realistic daytime natural light, clear visibility, balanced contrast, and restrained color grading.

Camera movement should be slow, stable, and smooth, with gentle transitions between scenes. The video should feel like one continuous supply-chain sequence rather than a fast montage. Leave clean negative space in parts of the frame so website headline text can remain readable.

Visual style: ultra-realistic, high-end corporate documentary, real infrastructure, real materials, realistic physics, natural greens, metal grays, navy shadows, and warm cream highlights.

Avoid readable text, visible brand logos, people as the main subject, distorted machinery, fake-looking vehicles, surreal transitions, oversaturated colors, dramatic weather, fast cuts, shaky camera, and artificial-looking visual effects.

Output: 16:9 horizontal, muted, suitable as an autoplay website hero background.

## Extension Prompt

Continue the same photorealistic commodity supply-chain video from its final frame.

The continuation should move from storage and inland logistics toward port infrastructure: cargo terminals, cranes, containers, bulk cargo handling, organized loading operations, and a large cargo vessel preparing for departure.

Maintain realistic daytime natural light, clear visibility, balanced contrast, slow stable camera movement, and premium corporate documentary pacing. The sequence should remain credible and operational, with no fast montage effect. Keep clean negative space in parts of the frame for website headline text.

Visual style: ultra-realistic, real infrastructure, real materials, realistic physics, restrained color grading, natural greens, metal grays, navy shadows, and warm cream highlights.

Avoid readable text, visible brand logos, people as the main subject, distorted machinery, fake-looking vehicles, surreal transitions, oversaturated colors, dramatic weather, fast cuts, shaky camera, and artificial-looking visual effects.

## Reject Candidates That Show

- Synthetic-looking cranes, vessels, containers, or commodity infrastructure.
- Illegible pseudo-text or fake brand marks.
- Artificial-looking visual effects or generic sci-fi UI.
- People as the main subject.
- Fast cuts, shaky camera, dramatic weather, or oversaturated colors.
- A composition that leaves no readable space for the hero headline.

## Fallback

If generated video quality is not credible enough, use clean real logistics footage or the current hero still and add the digital/on-chain layer in CSS/canvas.
