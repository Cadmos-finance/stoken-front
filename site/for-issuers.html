The opportunity

A new capital channel
not a replacement

Stoken connects commodity traders to a growing pool of institutional on-chain capital: stablecoin treasuries, DAO funds and crypto-native investors actively seeking real-economy yield.

This is not a replacement for your existing bank lines. It is a complementary channel that gives you access to capital that was previously out of reach.

The process

From request
to funded

Stoken has designed the issuance process to be fast, straightforward and confidential. From your first conversation with our team to capital in your account, the entire process takes 6–8 weeks.

Day 1

Facility Outline

Your team and Stoken agree on facility size, tenor, target rate and use of proceeds.

1–2 weeks

Credit Rating

A credit rating is assigned using Moody's baseline EDF-X dataset overlaid with Stoken's proprietary scoring methodology.

2–4 weeks

Pre-Marketing

Stoken approaches its investor network to gauge demand ahead of the issuance.

Parallel

Investor KYC

Investors complete Stoken's internal verification. No unverified capital participates.

Parallel

ised Docs

6–8 weeks

Facility Live

Smart contracts match the investor order book to your facility. Capital is deployed.
