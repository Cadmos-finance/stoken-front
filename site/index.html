From facility to token

See how capital moves through Stoken.

A facility is assessed, tokenized, allocated to verified investors, then serviced through coupon and repayment flows.

01

Facility outline

Facility terms are defined with the issuer: size, tenor, target rate, commodity flow and use of proceeds.

02

Rating assessment

Rating is assigned using Stoken scoring, Moody's RiskCalc, or both before investor capital is committed.

03

Tokenization

The facility is issued as an ERC-20 token structured as a ledger-based security under Swiss law.

04

Allocation

Verified investors allocate capital from whitelisted wallets. Smart-contract rules enforce access.

05

Coupon distribution

Coupon payments are received and distributed to token holders through the platform flow.

06

Repayment at maturity

At maturity, principal repayment returns to investors and the position is closed on-chain.
