What are stokens

The credit market behind the real
economy - tokenized

Commodity trade finance funds the movement of physical goods: oil, grain, metals, between producers and buyers.

These are short-term loans backed by real trade flows. Stoken tokenizes these facilities as rated debt instruments on Ethereum, giving on-chain investors direct access to an asset class that has historically been the exclusive domain of trade finance banks.

Stoken provides the working-capital facility Rated · Tokenized · ERC-20 on Ethereum USDC · USDT · 5–12% Yield
