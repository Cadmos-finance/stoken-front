What are stokens

The credit market behind the real economy — tokenized.

Commodity trade finance funds the movement of physical goods: oil, grain, metals, between producers and buyers. These are short-term loans backed by real trade flows. Stoken tokenizes these facilities as rated debt instruments on Ethereum, giving on-chain investors direct access to an asset class that has historically been the exclusive domain of trade finance banks.

Producer

Mines, farms, refineries originate physical commodities.

Trader

Moves physical goods between markets. Funded by Stoken.

End Buyer

Industrial consumer or processor receives the goods.
